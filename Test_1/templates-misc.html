Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ current_date }}

1. Information We Collect

Event Discovery collects minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Location Data: We detect your approximate location using your IP address to find events near you. This data is temporarily cached and not permanently stored.
  • Personal Information: Your first name, last name, and activity preferences are used only for the current search session.
  • Technical Data: Basic analytics such as page views and error logs to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Find relevant events in your area
  • Personalize event recommendations based on your interests
  • Improve our service quality and user experience

3. Data Retention

We follow a minimal data retention policy:

  • Search session data is retained for up to 24 hours
  • Location cache is retained for up to 7 days
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • EventBrite API: To fetch event information
  • OpenStreetMap: To display event locations on maps
  • IP Geolocation Services: To determine your approximate location

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure session management
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we have about your current session
  • Request deletion of your session data
  • Opt-out of our service at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: privacy@eventdiscovery.app
